The Hate U Give is a book and movie about a girl name Starr Carter who moves between two worlds. Her black neighborhood and the predominantly white prep school that she attends. For her neighborhood she's too white to be black and for her school she's too black to be white. Starr soons finds her two worlds clasing when her childhood bestfriend Khalil is shot and killed by a white cop in front of her. She struggles with the decision to speak up or kep her mouth shut. The Hate U Give discusses the issues of racism and police violence.
